Recommended Zoho Mapping

  • Jobs: custom module or Deals/Projects record.
  • Sub-jobs: child tasks linked to Jobs with assigned workers and acceptance status.
  • Assignment alerts: pending, accepted, or rejected confirmation records.
  • Issues: worker-to-supervisor help requests with open/resolved status.
  • Photos: Zoho attachments linked to each job.
  • Time logs: custom module for worker hours and cost control.
  • Materials: request records, then convert approved items into Zoho Books expenses or items.
  • Transport: driver trips linked to job, workers, pickup, drop, and GPS check-ins.
  • Temporary workers: outsource profiles with expiry date and revoked access status.
